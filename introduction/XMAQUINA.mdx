Humanoid robotics is emerging as a new asset class. Access, however, remains largely confined to private funding rounds and institutional capital. Retail participants and onchain capital have limited direct exposure to this sector

XMAQUINA introduces structured, onchain coordination to enable broader participation in the robotics market.
