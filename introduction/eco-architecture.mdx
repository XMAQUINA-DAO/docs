## **XMAQUINA DAO**

**Capital Allocation Layer**

The XMAQUINA DAO governs treasury deployment through structured, onchain governance.

Its responsibilities include:

- Deploying capital into private humanoid robotics companies
- Managing treasury structure and liquidity
- Approving ecosystem funding initiatives
- Coordinating long-term capital strategy

The DAO serves as the primary capital vehicle within the ecosystem.

## **Robotics Capital Markets (RCM) Protocol**

**Liquidity Layer**

The RCM Protocol introduces structured onchain liquidity to private humanoid robotics markets.

Its role within the ecosystem is to:

- Enable asset-specific exposure to humanoid robotics allocations
- Bring structured private positions onchain
- Facilitate 24/7 secondary market trading
- Generate protocol revenue for the DAO
- Reinforce demand for the DEUS token through liquidity pairing

Trading activity within RCM generates fees that flow to the DAO treasury and are governed by DEUS holders. By integrating liquidity with capital deployment, RCM connects private humanoid equity to decentralized market infrastructure.

## **Token-Based Governance**

**Coordination Layer**

DEUS functions as the coordination and governance mechanism across the ecosystem.

It enables:

- Proposal submission and voting
- Treasury direction and capital allocation approval
- Alignment between capital deployment, liquidity, and incubation initiatives
- Governance over treasury events, including liquidity events, protocol-generated resources, and staking incentives

Governance decisions are executed through transparent, structured processes.
