XMAQUINA is designed as infrastructure for coordinated participation in humanoid robotics markets.

Its architecture and governance operate under the following principles.

## **Focus**

XMAQUINA concentrates exclusively on humanoid robotics and closely related infrastructure.

This narrow mandate ensures capital, liquidity, and incubation efforts remain aligned with a single, defined sector rather than diffuse across broader themes.

## **Governance-Led Direction**

Strategic direction is determined through structured, onchain governance.

DEUS token holders collectively guide capital deployment, protocol evolution, and ecosystem development. Authority flows through governance rather than centralized control.

Transparency is treated as infrastructure, not marketing.
