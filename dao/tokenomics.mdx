The DEUS token has a fixed maximum supply and a structured allocation model designed to support long-term governance, treasury growth, and ecosystem expansion.

All supply mechanics and vesting parameters are defined below.

---

## **Supply Overview**

• Ticker: DEUS

• Maximum Supply: 1,000,000,000

• Total Supply: 1,000,000,000

• Inflation: None

• Token Standard: ERC20 (omnichain-compatible)

• Governance Model: veToken (xDEUS)

> The DEUS supply is capped at 1 billion tokens. No additional tokens may be minted beyond this maximum.

## **Allocation Breakdown**

The total supply is allocated across the following categories:

**DEUS Development Lab — 7.5%**

Allocated to the DAO’s internal innovation hub incubating robotics and Web3 ventures, enabling DAO-backed spin-outs that expand ecosystem reach and DEUS utility.

**Genesis Auctions — 23.24%**

Allocated to the community through the Genesis auction process, establishing initial governance participation and seeding the DAO treasury.

**DAO Treasury — 30%**

Reserved for long-term capital allocation under DAO governance. Deployment requires approved governance proposals.

**RCM Protocol — 2%**

Allocated to fund development, legal structuring, and expansion of the Robotics Capital Markets (RCM) Protocol.

**Liquidity & Ecosystem Growth — 8.26%**

Allocated for exchange listings, market-making, liquidity provisioning, and targeted ecosystem growth initiatives.

**Strategic Contributor Fundraise — 8%**

Allocated to strategic contributors who participated in the early fundraising round supporting initial DAO and infrastructure development.

**Advisors — 1%**

Allocated to advisors providing strategic guidance and ecosystem support to the DAO.

**Core Contributors — 12.5%**

Allocated to founding builders and core developers responsible for protocol development and transition to decentralized governance.

**Foundation — 7.5%**

Allocated to the foundation entity supporting operational execution, service providers, and ecosystem coordination under DAO oversight.
