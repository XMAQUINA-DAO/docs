XMAQUINA DAO is governed by DEUS token holders.

Governance determines:

- Treasury allocations
- Capital deployment decisions
- Ecosystem initiatives
- Protocol upgrades
- SubDAO mandates

The DAO operates under an on-chain governance framework powered by a vote-escrowed (veToken) model. Governance combines community proposal discussion, Snapshot signaling, and binding on-chain execution through Aragon.

## **Governance Structure**

The governance system includes:

- veToken-based voting (xDEUS)
- Optimistic dual governance mechanisms
- Northstar Council delegated proposals
- Community-led proposal pathways
- On-chain execution via Aragon

This structure balances speed, decentralization, and accountability.
