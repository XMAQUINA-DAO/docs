DEUS has a fixed maximum supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens.

Each allocation follows its own unlock and vesting schedule, which may include:

• Defined lock-up periods

• Linear vesting over time

• Immediate availability at TGE

Allocation-specific details are provided below.
