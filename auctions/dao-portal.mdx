After the transaction is confirmed on-chain, you can check your balance using:

- The Genesis Dashboard
- Your Wallet

The Genesis dashboard lets you visualize your portfolio of receipt tokens under the "Token Claim" tab, providing an overview of your holdings and their vesting details.
