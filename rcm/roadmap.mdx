## Where we are

The RCM Protocol is in **active legal structuring and design**. The core direction is set; mechanics, token structure, and the compliance framework are being finalized with counsel.

## Indicative phases

<Steps>
  <Step title="Foundations">
    Legal structuring with counsel, protocol design, and the first allocation framework. *(In progress.)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="First allocation">
    An initial robotics allocation: capital formation, SPV, a per-allocation token, and onchain liquidity paired against DEUS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Secondary market & liquidity">
    Structured onchain liquidity and price discovery; protocol fee flows to the DAO ecosystem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scale">
    Multiple allocations, broader eligible participation, and supporting tooling — subject to governance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  No dates are committed. Each phase depends on legal sign-off and DAO governance.
</Note>
